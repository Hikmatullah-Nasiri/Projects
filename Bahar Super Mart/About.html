History

After 2002, the charismatic Chairman and CEO of SMN Investment Limited, Hikmatullah Nasiri returned home to support private sector development in Afghanistan. His aim was to increase  the employment ratio, promote skill development programs and, introduce world class quality of products and services and the latest technology in Afghanistan.

The concept of formation of Bahar as a Superstore in which every type of local and international products to be introduced became a reality under the supervision of Engineer Atiqullah Sarwari. He is a well respected Afghan businessman, who ran his own chain of superstores in Canada. Engineer Atiqullah Sarwari gave shape and structure to the ambition of Mr. Nasiri  until we created the brand "Bahar".
The Bahar Team

Bahar Superstores is the retail division of the SMN Investment Group. It is the first chain of retail superstores in Afghanistan and plans to grow throughout the country. The first Bahar store opened its doors in 2006 and now the Bahar group boasts 6 superstores in Kabul, employing over 400 people. The retail group has a market capitalization of US$1.5 million, monthly. Such success would not be possible without our highly capable core team at the helm.

Return and Exchange Policy
No Cash refunds only Exchange of items will be entertained based of following conditions;
  1. Condition for Exchangeable Product:
    • Receipt must be presented for every exchangeable product.
    • Items returned for exchange will be acceptable if in the same condition as bought.
    • Items exchange could be entertained only on the same store locationand within three (3) days of purchased.
    • For only Electronics and Electrical products contains company Warrantee will be acceptable for exchange against manufacturing defects Otherwise, all electronics and Electrical products have check warrantee.
  1. Non-Exchangeable products:
  • Dairy Products (ice cream, yogurt, cheese)
  • Frozen & Fresh products
  • Special cut items (table covers, wire sold per meter, etc.)
  • Seasonal items (Mother’s Day, Valentine’s Day, Christmas, etc.)
  • Computer and related software, all Multimedia items (Audio, Video, Educational Trainings)
  • Lingerie and underwear
  • Cosmetics
  • Baby food and Milk
  • Personal Hygiene Products (shaver, nail clipper, trimmers)
  1. Discounted products will be exchanged at the same discounted price.

Please must note that Item short to expiries if expires or damages after sold will not be entertained against their above-mentioned specified time limit.
